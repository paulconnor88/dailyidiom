Privacy Policy

Daily Idiom Game is a free word game. We believe in keeping things simple, including your privacy.

What we collect

Almost nothing. Your game progress (trophies, XP, completed idioms) is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and we can't see it.

Analytics

We may use simple analytics to understand how many people play the game and which features they use. This data is anonymous and doesn't identify you personally.

No accounts, no tracking

We don't require sign-ups. We don't use cookies for tracking. We don't sell data (we don't have any to sell).

Third parties

The game is hosted on Netlify. When you share your results, you're using your device's native share functionality.

Changes

If this policy changes significantly, we'll update this page.
